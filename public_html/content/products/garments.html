Ready made Garments

Readymade garment sector unit:To Cater to the needs of growing population “SIRI” has several units which produce various types of readymade garments like Shirts, Children wears, Ladies Garments, Hospital Uniforms, School Uniforms.

About 358 SHG members are working in 23 production units across Dakshina Kannada, Dharwad and Haveri Districts.

The training to the members is provided by Rural Development and Self Employment Training Institute (RUDSETI).

Necessary Trainings, Machinery and the Raw materials needed for production are provided by SIRI.
